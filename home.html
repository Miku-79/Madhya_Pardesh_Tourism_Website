Why Explore Madhya Pradesh

Rich Heritage & Ancient Monuments

Discover UNESCO World Heritage sites like Khajuraho Temples, Sanchi Stupa, and the ancient city of Mandu that showcase the rich cultural heritage of India.

Breathtaking Natural Beauty

Experience the mesmerizing Marble Rocks of Bhedaghat, majestic waterfalls like Dhuandhar, and pristine landscapes that make MP a paradise for nature lovers.

Diverse Wildlife

Explore some of India's finest national parks like Kanha, Bandhavgarh, and Pench, home to tigers, leopards, and hundreds of bird species in their natural habitat.

Vibrant Culture & Traditions

Immerse yourself in the colorful tribal cultures, traditional arts and crafts, and the warm hospitality that defines the heart of Incredible India.

Explore Wildlife

Kanha National Park

Kanha Tiger Reserve, also known as Kanha National Park, is one of the finest wildlife areas in the world. It is the inspiration behind Rudyard Kipling's "The Jungle Book".

Bandhavgarh National Park

Known for the highest density of tigers in India, Bandhavgarh offers excellent opportunities for wildlife enthusiasts to spot the majestic Royal Bengal Tigers.

Pench Tiger Reserve

Nestled in the lower southern reaches of the Satpura hills, Pench National Park is named after the river Pench which flows through the park.
